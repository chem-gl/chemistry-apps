CADMA-Chem Protocol

CADMAPy

Score candidate molecules against a disease-specific reference drug family using ADMET properties, toxicity and synthetic accessibility — prioritising the best candidates for synthesis and further evaluation.

  1. Select a disease baseline from the pre-built reference sets (Neuro or RETT) or create a custom family.
  2. Upload candidate CSVs exported from Smile-it, Toxicity Properties and SA Score, or a single combined CSV.
  3. Rank and compare candidates by selection score against the reference band — export the results as CSV.
